Chicken Adobo

The famous Chicken Adobo from the Philippines

Classic Chicken Adobo recipe from the Philippines that's simple to make and loved by all who tries it. It has been modified to be a bit saucier than traditional adobo and is delicious. Served over rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 2/3 cup of low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup of white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons of black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
  3. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside
  4. Add onion and garlic to the skillet; cook until softened and brown, about 6 minutes.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar and season with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf.
  6. Return chicken to pan, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low,cover,and simmer until chicken is tender and cooked through,35 to 40 minutes.
